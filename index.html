Image Classification of Plant Leaves

I successfully identified 99 plant species using machine learning techniques, focusing on leaf features such as shape, margin, and texture. By applying methods like Naive Bayes, SVM, Logistic Regression, k-NN, and Linear Discriminant Analysis, I achieved notable results. Ultimately, Logistic Regression yielded the highest accuracy at around 99%.
